Consulting firm specializing in hydrogeological and geo-environmental services. We provide comprehensive solutions for the exploration, characterization, and management of water and mineral resources, combining advanced technology, professional expertise, and a sustainable approach.

About us.

JB GEO was founded in 2022 amid growing mining activity in northwestern Argentina. Our goal is to provide comprehensive hydrogeological consulting services, drawing on the experience of our founders and the application of innovative technologies. We support companies and organizations in the exploration, quantification, and sustainable management of water resources, offering reliable and sustainable water resource management solutions that deliver value.

Services

Geophysical prospecting

We have equipment to perform vertical electrical surveys and electrical profiling of wells.

Sampling and integration of information

Sampling and generation of statistical information on the variables that act in a hydrogeological basin.

Hydraulic parameters of wells and aquifers

Conducting and interpreting pumping tests to assess the hydraulic parameters of wells and aquifers.

Geographic Information Systems and Software

Application of software and Geographic Information Systems in geo-environmental, hydrogeological, and mining studies, using geological mapping and geoprocessing techniques with 2D and 3D representation, as well as various remote sensing tools.

Quantification and classification of water resources

We assess and organize water availability in a region through technical and scientific studies. This process allows us to identify the volume, quality, and types of water resources, providing the information necessary to plan for their efficient and sustainable use.

Development of conceptual, hydrodynamic, and balance models

Development of geostatistical and hydrodynamic modeling of basins. Water load and balance studies.

Comprehensive solutions and support for productive development

We provide comprehensive services ranging from initial exploration to the production stage. We support our clients in the planning, implementation, and optimization of mining and environmental projects, contributing technical expertise and strategic vision to ensure sustainable results.
