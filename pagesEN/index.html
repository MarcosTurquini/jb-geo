Who We Are

Services

Geophysical Prospecting

We have equipment to perform Vertical Electrical Soundings and Electric Well Logging.

Sampling and Information Integration

Sampling and generation of statistical information for variables acting within a hydrogeological basin.

Topography and Geodesy with RTK Drones and GPS

We conduct high-precision topographic surveys using RTK drones and geodetic GPS equipment. These studies allow for the generation of digital terrain models, orthomosaics, contour lines, volume calculations, and detailed planialtimetry, fundamental for engineering, construction control, and mine planning.

Hydraulic Parameters of Wells and Aquifers

Execution and interpretation of pumping tests for the assessment of hydraulic parameters of wells and aquifers.

Well Filming and Diagnosis

Internal inspection with a camera to evaluate the well's condition, detect obstructions or damage, and provide a clear diagnosis for its maintenance and optimization.

Software and Geographic Information Systems

Application of software and Geographic Information Systems in geo-environmental, hydrogeological, and mining studies, using geological mapping techniques and geoprocessing with 2D and 3D representation, as well as various remote sensing tools.

Quantification and Classification of Water Resources

We evaluate and organize water availability in a region through technical and scientific studies. This process identifies volumes, quality, and types of water resources, providing the necessary information to plan their efficient and sustainable use.

Development of Conceptual, Hydrodynamic, and Balance Models

Development of geostatistical and hydrodynamic basin models. Recharge and water balance studies.

Comprehensive Solutions and Support in Productive Development

We provide a comprehensive service covering from initial exploration to the production stage. We accompany our clients in the planning, implementation, and optimization of mining and environmental projects, providing technical experience and a strategic vision to guarantee sustainable results.

Legal and Regulatory Advisory

We provide support in permit management, technical submissions to regulatory authorities, and compliance with environmental and water regulations. Our team assists companies in preparing impact reports, technical defenses, and legal procedures related to the exploration and exploitation of natural resources.
