Solutions

Geophysical Prospecting

We have in-house equipment to perform Vertical Electrical Soundings (VES) and electrical well logging for subsurface characterization and hydrogeological assessment.

Sampling and Data Integration

Sampling programs and generation of statistical datasets for the analysis and integration of key variables operating within a hydrogeological basin.

Topography and Geodesy with RTK Drones and GPS

We conduct high-precision topographic surveys using RTK drones and geodetic GPS equipment. These studies enable the generation of digital terrain models (DTMs), orthomosaics, contour lines, volume calculations, and detailed planimetry, essential for engineering design, construction control, and mine planning.

Hydraulic Parameters of Wells and Aquifers

Execution and interpretation of pumping tests to assess the hydraulic parameters of wells and aquifers, supporting reliable hydrogeological modeling and water resource management.

Well Filming and Diagnosis

Internal well inspections using downhole cameras to evaluate well condition, identify obstructions or structural damage, and provide clear diagnostics for maintenance, rehabilitation, and operational optimization.

Software and Geographic Information Systems

Application of specialized software and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) for geo-environmental, hydrogeological, and mining studies, including geological mapping, 2D and 3D geoprocessing, and the integration of remote sensing tools.

Quantification and Classification of Water Resources

We assess and structure water availability within a region using technical and scientific methodologies. This process identifies water volumes, quality, and resource types, providing the essential information required for efficient planning and sustainable development of projects.

Development of Conceptual, Hydrodynamic, and Balance Models

Development of conceptual, geostatistical, and hydrodynamic basin models, including groundwater recharge assessment and water balance studies, to support informed decision-making and sustainable resource management.

Comprehensive Solutions and Support in Productive Development

We deliver end-to-end services covering the entire project lifecycle, from early-stage exploration to production. We support our clients through planning, implementation, and optimization of mining and environmental projects, combining technical expertise with strategic insight to ensure sustainable outcomes.

Legal and Regulatory Advisory

We provide technical support for permitting processes, regulatory submissions, and compliance with environmental and water regulations. Our team assists in the preparation of environmental impact reports, technical defenses, and regulatory documentation related to the exploration and exploitation of natural resources.
