Desde 2022

JB GEO

It emerged within the framework of sustainable growth in mining activity, to offer comprehensive hydrogeological consulting based on the experience of its founding partners and the development of new technologies and methods for the exploration, quantification, and control of water resources in general. With the challenge of becoming a comprehensive, innovative, and efficient solution for our clients, JB GEO offers a responsible and value-added service through the use of advanced methodologies and a highly trained and committed team.

  • Scientific rigor
  • Innovation and technology management
  • Management and planning skillsn
  • Working in adverse conditions
  • Commitment to safety and the environment
  • Analytical and observational skills
